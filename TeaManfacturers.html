Dilmah

Single Origin Tea from Sri Lanka, one of the finest tea nations in the world, Dilmah Tea Sri Lanka was the first producer-owned tea brand to deliver tea 'selected, mastered and packaged' at origin. Dilmah offered tea that is freshly packaged at origin and thus rich in flavor and natural goodness, compared to the multi-origin blends that monopolised store shelves. 300,000 square feet of built-up space on 4 acres, 1200 workers, over 70 modern tea packing machines and rigorous quality assurance requirements are employed at the Dilmah headquarters in Peliyagoda to reliably produce the finest cup of tea. Dilmah is the only entirely vertically integrated tea firm that operates some of Sri Lanka's finest tea gardens, modern factories, printing and packaging machines, making it one of the nation's leading premium tea brands.
